# CEREX PLATFORM – PROJECT BRAIN

## Vision

Cerex Platform is a production-grade multi-tenant PropTech SaaS platform designed to improve real estate lead conversion through buyer personalization, AI-assisted apartment visualization, behavioral analytics, and sales intelligence.

The goal is **not** to build another real estate website.

The goal is to build an operating system for real estate developers.

Every developer uses the same backend.

Every project has its own branding, apartments, AI knowledge, furniture library, analytics and CRM data.

---

## Core Philosophy

The buyer should feel like they are designing **their future home**, not browsing a brochure.

The developer should receive **buyer intelligence**, not just leads.

The sales team should know what matters to the buyer before making the first call.

## AI Philosophy

AI never writes directly to the database.

AI never decides business rules.

AI only returns structured JSON.

Business logic belongs in backend services.

---

## Viewer Philosophy

The viewer renders apartment models.

AI does not generate geometry.

AI recommends furniture.

The backend validates placement.

The frontend renders the final layout.

---

## Multi-Tenant Rules

One backend.

One database.

Unlimited developers.

Unlimited projects.

Every request belongs to exactly one project.

Everything is project-aware.

No project-specific code.

---

## Engineering Rules

Controllers stay thin.

Business logic belongs in services.

No duplicate code.

No hardcoded developer data.

No direct OpenAI calls from controllers.

Everything uses DTOs.

Everything is strongly typed.

Events are immutable.

Everything is scalable.

Everything is production-ready.

Never break existing APIs without approval.

---

## Long-Term Goal

Cerex should become the operating system for residential real estate sales by combining buyer personalization, AI recommendations, 3D visualization, analytics, CRM, and marketing automation into one unified platform.
